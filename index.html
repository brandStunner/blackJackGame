Rules

  • Draw three random cards,choosing two first
  • If the value of the cards are below 21, you keep drawing the card
  • If the cards sum up to exactly 21, you hit the BlackJact and are the biggest winner
  • If you play in turns, the person with third card sum closest to 21, but not above 21 is the winner
  • If your cards sum over 21 you are out of the game
